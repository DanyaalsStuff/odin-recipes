Best Carrots!

Description:

Glazed carrots are a simple and versatile side dish that combines sweet and savory flavors. Made by simmering carrots until tender and then coating them in a glossy glaze of butter, brown sugar, and spices, they offer a delightful contrast of textures with their soft interior and caramelized edges. Often seasoned with a pinch of salt and pepper, glazed carrots complement a variety of main courses, making them a popular choice for both weeknight meals and special occasions.

Ingredients:

  • Carrots
  • Butter
  • Brown Sugar
  • Seasoniiiing

Steps:

  1. Cook the carrots.
  2. Glaze the carrots.
