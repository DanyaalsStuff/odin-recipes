Best Pancakes!

Description:

Pancakes, a beloved breakfast staple, are fluffy, round cakes made from a batter of flour, eggs, milk, and a leavening agent like baking powder. Cooked on a hot griddle or pan, they develop a golden brown exterior and a light, airy interior filled with tiny bubbles. Depending on preference, they can be thin and delicate or thick and decadent. Typically enjoyed for breakfast, pancakes are often drizzled with maple syrup and dotted with butter, but can also be dressed up with fresh fruit, whipped cream, or even savory ingredients like cheese and herbs.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Mix flour, eggs, milk, butter, sugar baking powder and salt together.
  2. Heat a griddle over low heat. Scoop 1/4 cup of batter onto the griddle and cook until edges are dry, 3-4 minutes. Flip and cook for another 2-3 minutes. Repeat with remaining batter.
